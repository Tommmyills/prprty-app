## Getting Started

When you first open The Closer, you will see the **Command Center** - your central hub for managing all active transactions. The app features a premium dark theme with glass-effect cards for an elegant, professional experience.

### Key Icons to Know

- **Sun Icon** - Daily Digest (your morning briefing)
- **Chat Bubble Icon** - AI Assistant (ask any real estate question)
- **Gear Icon** - Settings
- **Red Badge** - Number of overdue deadlines requiring attention

---

## Command Center (Dashboard)

The Command Center is your home base. Here you can see:

### Next Required Action
At the top, you will see your most urgent action item with its deadline. Tap this card to go directly to the transaction timeline.

- **Gold highlight** = Action is upcoming
- **Red highlight** = Action is overdue

### Quick Actions Menu
Six main features accessible with one tap:

1. **Upload Contract** - Extract deadlines from contracts using AI
2. **Transaction Timeline** - View and track all milestones
3. **AI Email Assistant** - Generate professional emails instantly
4. **Weekly Summary** - Create client-ready status reports
5. **Live Negotiation Assistant** - Real-time support during calls
6. **Add Deal Context** - Save deal information for negotiations

### Active Transactions
Below the Quick Actions, you will see all your active transactions. Each card shows:

- Property address
- Client name and price
- Status indicator (Active/Overdue/Completed)
- Number of AI-extracted deadlines (if applicable)

**Tap any transaction** to view its detailed timeline.

## Upload Contract

This powerful feature uses AI to automatically extract all critical deadlines from your contracts.

### How to Use

1. Tap **Upload Contract** from the Command Center
2. Tap the upload area to select a PDF or image file
3. Once uploaded, you will see a green "Upload Successful" confirmation
4. Tap **Extract Critical Dates** to begin AI analysis
5. Wait for the AI to process (you will see a scanning animation)
6. Review the extracted information:
   - Contract Summary (2-3 sentence overview)
   - Next Required Action
   - Property details (address, client name, price)
   - All extracted deadlines in chronological order

### What Gets Extracted

- Earnest money due date
- Inspection period end
- Appraisal deadline
- Loan commitment date
- Closing/settlement date
- Repair request deadline
- HOA document deadlines
- Title commitment deadline
- All contingency periods
- Seller response deadlines

### After Extraction

- **Edit any deadline** by tapping on it
- **Delete a deadline** by long-pressing on it
- Tap **Save to Transactions** to add this deal to your active transactions

## Transaction Timeline

The Timeline gives you a visual map of every milestone in your transaction.

### Understanding the Timeline

Each milestone shows as a colored node:

- **Green** = Completed
- **Cyan (pulsing)** = Current/In Progress
- **Red (pulsing)** = Overdue - needs immediate attention
- **Gray** = Upcoming

### How to Use

1. Navigate to **Transaction Timeline** from the Command Center
2. View the Contract Summary at the top (if AI-extracted)
3. **Tap any milestone** to expand its details:
   - Full deadline date
   - Status badge
   - Suggested next step
   - Mark as Completed button

### Marking Milestones Complete

1. Tap the milestone to expand it
2. Tap **Mark as Completed**
3. The node turns green and the timeline updates

## AI Email Assistant

Generate professional, polished emails in seconds for any transaction scenario.

### How to Use

1. Tap **AI Email Assistant** from the Command Center
2. Select an **Email Category**:
   - Inspection Scheduling
   - Appraisal Request
   - Lender Follow-up
   - Title Update
   - Client Update
3. Choose your **Tone**:
   - Professional (formal business tone)
   - Friendly (warm and approachable)
   - Urgent (emphasizes time-sensitivity)
4. Tap **Generate Email**
5. Review the generated email
6. Tap **Copy Email** to copy to your clipboard
7. Paste into your email app and send

### Tips

- The email automatically includes your transaction details (property address, client name, price)
- Use **Regenerate** if you want a different version
- Copy button turns green when successfully copied

## Live Negotiation Assistant

Real-time decision support during phone negotiations - like having a coach in your ear.

### Before Your Call

1. Tap **Live Negotiation Assistant** from the Command Center
2. Select an **Active Deal Context** from the dropdown (or create one)
3. Optionally add session-specific notes in the Deal Memory panel:
   - Add quick notes about key facts
   - Paste relevant emails or messages
   - Upload documents (inspection reports, etc.)
   - Take photos of relevant documents

### During Your Call

1. Tap **Start** before beginning your negotiation
2. Put your phone on speaker
3. The assistant listens for negotiation keywords:
   - Price, counteroffer, offer
   - Credit, concession
   - Inspection, repairs
   - Appraisal, financing
   - Contingency, deadline, closing date
4. When keywords are detected, you will receive:
   - **Suggested Approach** (1-2 sentences)
   - **Relevant Context** from your notes (if applicable)
   - **Suggested Phrasing** (exact words you can use)

### After Your Call

1. Tap **Stop** to end the session
2. Review any suggestions you received
3. Session notes are saved automatically

### Important Notes

- This is decision support, not advice - use your own judgment
- The assistant only uses information YOU provide
- Does not provide legal, financial, or professional advice

## Deal Context Memory

Save and organize deal information that persists across all your sessions.

### Creating a Deal Context

1. Tap **Add Deal Context** from the Command Center
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Deal Name/Label** (e.g., "123 Maple St - Buyer Offer Round 2")
   - **Property Address**
   - **Key Notes** (important facts, numbers, terms)
   - **Paste Email/Message** (full email threads for reference)
   - **Upload Documents** (PDFs, images)
3. Tap **Save**

### Using Deal Contexts

- Select your saved context in the Live Negotiation Assistant
- All your saved information becomes available during the session
- Edit or update contexts anytime from the Dashboard

## AI Assistant

Your personal real estate expert available 24/7 - "The Realtor's Realtor."

### How to Access

Tap the **Chat Bubble icon** in the Dashboard header.

### Voice Features

- **Tap the purple microphone** to speak your question
- **Voice responses** in a clear British accent
- Fully hands-free operation

### What You Can Ask

**Transaction Questions:**
- "What should I do today?"
- "Explain the inspection deadline"
- "Summarize this contract simply"
- "How many days until closing?"

**Business Questions:**
- "How do I handle a lowball offer?"
- "Draft a message to my buyer"
- "Create a listing presentation outline"
- "Help me with commission calculations"

**Any Real Estate Topic:**
- Contracts and contingencies
- Negotiation strategies
- Client management
- Marketing ideas
- Market analysis

### Response Types

Messages are color-coded:
- **Blue** = Guidance and how-to advice
- **Amber** = Warnings and cautions
- **Red** = Deadline-related alerts
- **Green** = Reassurance messages

### Editor Window Features

- **Pause** - Temporarily stop new AI suggestions
- **Mute** - Disable AI text updates while you type
- **Recenter** - Refocus the conversation without clearing text
- **Clear** - Start fresh

### Conversation History

- Tap the **clock icon** to view saved conversations
- All chats are automatically saved
- Tap any past conversation to continue it
- Swipe left to delete old conversations

## Tips for Success

### Daily Routine

1. **Morning**: Check your Daily Digest to see what needs attention
2. **Before calls**: Load deal context in the Live Negotiation Assistant
3. **After calls**: Update transaction timelines and mark completed items
4. **End of week**: Generate Weekly Summaries for all clients

### Best Practices

- **Upload contracts immediately** after signing to capture all deadlines
- **Use the AI Assistant** instead of searching online - it knows real estate
- **Keep Deal Contexts updated** for better negotiation support
- **Mark milestones complete** as they happen to maintain accurate tracking

### Staying Organized

- Review overdue items (red badges) first thing each day
- Use color-coded status to quickly identify deals needing attention
- Generate Weekly Summaries every Friday to keep clients informed
